IMU Sensor

Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU) sensor detects and records movements of the body, such as gait patterns. It plays a vital role in identifying abnormalities.

Pen Display

A pen display is a digital device that allows users to draw directly on a screen using a stylus. In the context of neurodegenerative disease detection, pen displays are used to capture changes in handwriting or drawing patterns.

NDD

Neurodegenerative diseases are conditions characterized by the progressive degeneration of nerve cells in the brain or spinal cord. Early detection of these diseases is crucial for initiating appropriate treatment.
